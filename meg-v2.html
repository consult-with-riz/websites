About

Dr. Meghaan Lurtz is a global expert on the psychology of financial planning and the intersection of money and wellbeing. A Partner at Shaping Wealth, she teaches Financial Psychology at Columbia University and advanced financial planning at Kansas State, and is a past president of the Financial Therapy Association. She writes at Kitces.com and in her newsletter, (Less) Lonely Money.
